The Swipe Feature


On Unified, connecting with like-minded students has never been easier! Our swipe and Match feature lets you effortlessly form meaningful connections with fellow students.

Imagine walking into a massive lecture hall, scanning the room, and instantly spotting someone who shares your interests, goals, or even your favourite study spot - That's the power of Unified TSN!


  • Swipe right to send friend request
  • Swipe left to see more students
  • Filter by role, gender, university, city and course

Embrace the power of the Unified TSN Marketplace and unlock the potential of your student-owned business!

  • Instant chat answers from course professionals > long wait for formal responses from busy lecturers

Frequently asked questions

What is Unified TSN?
Unified TSN is an iOS/Android social networking platform for university students to connect with other students and tutors. Unified TSN will also host a marketplace for all and only student-owned businesses

Can I connect with students in other universities?
For sure! You can filter students by City, University or Course to find the exact people you are looking for!
Can I sell anything on the marketplace?
The marketplace exists to promote all businesses and side hustles started by university students. On the marketplace you can also open shop pages to sell your old clothes, books and other items. If it can be sold in a shop, you can sell it on Unified!
How do I connect with Tutors?
If you want to connect with tutors, you can via the subscription 'Tutor Access'. Through this subscription you can download resources (webinars, documents, power-points, links, videos) directly from any and all tutors' profiles.

You can also connect with all tutors on the app and ask them whatever questions are troubling you and award the tutors with points when they answer your queries.If you require hourly tutoring, you can discuss this with the tutors directly. We don't charge the tutors a commission so their prices can remain student friendly!
